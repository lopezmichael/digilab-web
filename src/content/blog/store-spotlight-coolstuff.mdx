This month we're spotlighting CoolStuffGames Jacksonville — one of Florida's most active Digimon TCG scenes.

## The Store

CoolStuffGames has been running Digimon events since the game launched. Their Jacksonville location hosts weekly locals every Saturday at 2 PM.

## By the Numbers

- **50+ tournaments** tracked on DigiLab
- **120+ unique players** in their community
- **Average attendance**: 16 players per event

## What Makes Them Special

The Jacksonville community has built something great:

- Active Discord server for coordination
- New player friendly — regulars help teach the game
- Consistent prize support
