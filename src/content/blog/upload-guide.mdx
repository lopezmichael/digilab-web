Anyone can upload tournament results to DigiLab. Here's how.

## What You'll Need

- Screenshots of final standings from Bandai TCG+
- Tournament details (store, date, player count)

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Go to Upload Results

Open the app and navigate to the Upload Results tab in the sidebar.

### 2. Fill in Tournament Details

- Select your store from the dropdown
- Choose the tournament date
- Enter total players and rounds

### 3. Upload Screenshots

Take clear screenshots of the standings from Bandai TCG+. Our OCR system will extract:

- Player names
- Placements
- Member numbers

### 4. Review and Submit

Check the extracted data for any OCR errors. You can also assign deck archetypes if you know what players were running.

## Tips for Best Results

- Make sure placements are visible in screenshots
- Capture all pages if standings span multiple screens
- Higher resolution = better OCR accuracy

Results go live immediately after submission!
