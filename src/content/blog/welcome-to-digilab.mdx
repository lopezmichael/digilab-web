Welcome to DigiLab! We're building a tournament tracker focused on your local Digimon TCG scene.

## What is DigiLab?

DigiLab is a community-built platform that helps you:

- **Track your tournament history** — See all your results in one place
- **Follow your local meta** — Know what decks are winning at your stores
- **Compare with your scene** — Ratings that reflect who you actually play against

## Why Scene-Based?

Unlike global meta trackers, DigiLab focuses on regional communities. Your rating reflects your performance against the players you actually face at locals — not some abstract global average.

## Getting Started

1. Find your scene using the scene selector
2. Search for your name in the Players tab
3. Start tracking your results

We're excited to grow this with the community. See you at locals!
