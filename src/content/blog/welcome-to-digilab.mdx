DigiLab is a tournament tracking platform for the Digimon TCG competitive scene. If you play locals, this is for you.

## What DigiLab Does

DigiLab tracks tournament results across local game stores and calculates player ratings based on your performance. Everything is organized by **scene**, the regional community you actually play in.

Player ratings are Elo-based, calculated from your tournament results. Every event your scene has run is recorded with full standings. You can see which decks are winning at your locals and find stores running Digimon TCG events near you.

![DigiLab scene overview](/images/blog/digilab-overviewscreenshot.png)

## Community-Powered Results

DigiLab is powered by the community. Anyone can upload tournament results directly from Bandai TCG+ app screenshots. Take a screenshot of your tournament standings, upload it to DigiLab, and the results get processed automatically. Ratings update, standings are recorded, and the meta data gets richer.

![Uploading results to DigiLab](/images/blog/digilab-uploadscreenshot.png)

## Why Scene-Based?

Most competitive trackers focus on global data. DigiLab focuses on *your* community. Your rating reflects how you perform against the players you actually face at locals, not some abstract global average.

The meta at your local store is different from the meta online. The player who wins your Saturday locals every week deserves recognition for that.

## Get Started

1. Head to [app.digilab.cards](https://app.digilab.cards)
2. Find your scene using the scene selector
3. Look up your name in the Players tab

If your scene isn't on DigiLab yet, join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/ABcjha7bHk) and let us know. We'll get you set up.
